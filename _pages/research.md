## Research Overview

My research focuses on **Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)** for **mental health analysis via social media**, emphasizing **trustworthy**, **interpretable**, and **domain-aware** deep learning.  
I also explore cross-disciplinary applications of AI in healthcare and ethics.

## Research Themes

| Area | Description |
|:--|:--|
| **Explainable AI (XAI)** | Developing interpretable and trustworthy neural models, especially in high-stakes healthcare domains. |
| **Mental Health via Social Media** | Designing models to infer mental health indicators (e.g., depression, stress) from textual data while ensuring transparency and ethical compliance. |
| **Knowledge-Aware Deep Learning** | Integrating clinical ontologies and knowledge graphs into neural architectures to enhance explainability. |
| **AI Ethics & Safety** | Investigating fairness, bias mitigation, and epistemological challenges in AI decision systems. |
| **Medical Imaging & Transfer Learning** | Leveraging domain-pretrained models (e.g., RadImageNet) for robust and interpretable healthcare diagnostics. |

---

## Current Research Directions

- Causal and knowledge-based attention mechanisms for **interpretable social media analysis**
- **Explainability-aware training frameworks** that maintain differentiability during optimization
- **Cross-modal explainability** between text and medical data
- Frameworks for **evaluating and benchmarking XAI quality** in mental healthcare applications
